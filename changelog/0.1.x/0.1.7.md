# 0.1.7 — 2026-05-24

- **`stripHtml`** — two duplicate definitions in `gbif-get-dataset.tool.ts` and `gbif-search-datasets.tool.ts` consolidated into a shared `src/mcp-server/tools/utils.ts`.
